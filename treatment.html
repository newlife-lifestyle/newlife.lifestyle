Our Clinical Solutions

Aesthetic Medicine

Aesthetic medicine involves high-technology skin care that represents the fusion of healthcare and beauty services. These services involve the use of lasers or other engery based devices without surgery. With us, you can have the benefits of cosmetic procedures without down time.

Anti-Aging Medicine

Anti-aging is the new era of medical science to promote our health with ultimate aim not only to slow down the aging process in our bodies but also to improve quality of our lives. To achieve this, it applies preventive medicine and regenerative medicine. Preventive medicine means early detection of disease through medical check-up. Regenerative medicine works on the principles of giving our bodies the right diet, appropriate hormonal replacement and supplements to improve our cells' biological age and enhance our immune system.

Gene Test

Gene is the blueprint of life. It maps out our individual characteristics. Gene Test is here to help you find out SNP or particular mal condition caused in nucleotide, which results in different genetic traits that may cause some diseases or affect some physical system. Know your risk of developing cancers, such as prostate cancer, breast cancer and colon cancer; the risk of developing some frequently found diseases, such as cardiovascular disease, high cholesterol, hypertension, diabetes and Alzheimer's disease so that you can design a customized diet, lifestle and supplement that helps reduce these risks.


Our Treatments

Laser

Laser treatment is a precise, predictable and safe procedure. We use advanced laser technology for skin rejuvenation, to reduce wrinkles, minimise pore size, remove uneven pigmentation, age spots, warts, moles, cysts and spider vein. It is also used for permanent hair reduction and for tattoo removal.

a) Acne treatment
b) Photo Rejuvenation : light treatment to revitalize skin.

Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP)

To stimulate cells to regenerate new tissues. This is the latest technology applied in aesthetic field, and highly effective ageing prevention.

Thread Lift

Thread lift is a minimally invasive cosmetique enhancement technique that lifts sagging skin on the face and neck.

Botox

Tiny injections of botox can smooth out frown lines, crow's feet and horizontal forehead lines as well as hyperhidrosis (excessive sweating), lift brows, contour square jawlines (masseter) and even elevate nasal tip.

Dermal Fillers

This is used to smoothen and fill out deep furrows, deep wrinkles, lines and to plump-up lips.

Chemical Peels

We perform various depths of chemical peels customized to each patient's needs. The peels are used to improve the appearance of wrinkles, brown spots, acne and general skin texture.

Mesotherapy

Commonly used to reduce cellulite and unsightly fat deposits. Also for skin rejuvenation.

Microdermabrasion

Procedure is designed to soften fine wrinkles, smooth scarring and rejuvenate skin by removing the top most layer of dead skin and promoting unblemished skin growth.

Radio Frequency

Non-invasive treatment of cellulite; inducing collagen remodelling and shrinkage for firmer looking skin.

High Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU)

HIFU devices are used to induce subcutaneous tissue contraction for Non Invasive Facelift and to treat subcutaneous adipose tissue for the purposes of Body Contouring. It is also used with lower energy levels, for eyebrow lifts.

Oxygen Facial

The use of oxygen facial will help rejuvenate the skin, showing off healthier skin.
Pressurised oxygen is delivered to the face and skin cells are replenished.
Since oxygen is a bactericide that is all natural, the use of oxygen on the skin will cool and calm it, as well as destroy anaerobic bacteria.
